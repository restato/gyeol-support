# Gyeol Support Guide

## The model will not download

- Connect to Wi-Fi.
- Confirm that at least 3 GB of free storage is available.
- Keep the download active or return later; iOS can continue the background transfer.
- If verification fails, choose **Try download again**.

## Reflection is unavailable

Writing and saved entries remain available without Gemma 4. Open Settings and confirm that the on-device model is ready, then choose **Read again**.

## Delete data

Open Settings from the gear button on Today. Journals and the model have separate deletion controls.

## Privacy

Gyeol has no account or cloud sync. Journal content remains on the device and is not available to the developer.

For unresolved issues, open an issue without including private journal text.
